Integrated Strategy Overview

Ian, here's the situation and the plan. Your quiz funnel is actually working better than ever -- quiz sales are up 83% since 2022. The problem isn't traffic. It's what happens after someone takes the quiz.

The product ladder is broken. In 2022, mid-tier sales were 185% of quiz sales volume (driven by multiple traffic sources beyond the quiz). Today that ratio is just 27%. That single metric explains the entire revenue decline. Fix the ladder, fix the business.

The plan has three synchronized moves, designed to work together:

Move 1: Re-permission 400K+ dormant contacts. You have over 400,000 dormant contacts in Ontraport — not just dormant contacts, but everyone who's gone cold. Phase 1: Send an ethical gift ("Blueprintified Sex Techniques" 3-part video series) + a short survey to re-engage and segment them. Phase 2: Based on survey responses, deliver targeted offers matched to their actual needs. Conservative projection: $40K. Realistic: $60-80K. Optimistic: $100-1400K+ over 90 days.

Move 2: Rebuild the content engine. Censorship-safe content on X driving quiz signups. 22 posts ready for the first 2 weeks. Every piece targets a specific Blueprint type and funnels to the quiz. This refills the top while we fix the middle.

Move 3: Fix the funnel permanently. The Financial Analyst identified that restoring mid-tier sales ratios alone is worth $1.3M annually. This means rebuilding the post-quiz email sequences, optimizing the Pleasure Playbook sales page, and creating a clear path from $17 to $69 to $390.

Why this will work: We're not guessing. We have 60,178 transactions of data showing exactly where the funnel broke and exactly what sales ratios looked like when it was working. We're reverse-engineering 2022's success with 2026's tools. The audience is already in your CRM — 400K+ contacts. We just need to talk to them again, starting with a genuine gift, not a pitch.
